Mission

Turn curiosity into hands-on confidence.

Spark Gears creates low-barrier STEM experiences where students can build, code, test, present, and learn by doing. The goal is simple: make advanced technology feel less distant, less intimidating, and more open.

What We Build Around

  • Student-led teaching and mentoring
  • Free or low-cost access to tools and learning materials
  • Workshops that end with something real students can demo
  • Community events designed for beginners and returning builders

Gearbox Library

Programs students can walk into and actually build with.

Gearbox Library is the collection of repeatable Spark Gears programs: hackathons, library stations, workshops, coding bootcamps, and open build events that can be adapted for schools, libraries, and learning centers.

01

Code Crush Hackathons

24-hour game and project competitions with workshops in Python, Java, AI/ML, CAD, marketing, and pitching.

02

Library STEM Stations

Drop-in tables for robot driving, 3D printing, programming, AI experiments, and hardware demos.

03

Robotics Bootcamps

Beginner sessions covering robot parts, 3D modeling, programming concepts, design, outreach, and live demos.

04

Open Build Events

Guided sessions where students prototype, debug, document, and present small engineering projects.
